LEGAL BRIEF - Johnson v. TechCorp 2024
Claim: Wrongful termination based on race under Title VII.
Precedents: McDonnell Douglas Corp. v. Green 411 U.S. 792 (1973) burden-shifting framework.
Texas Dept. of Community Affairs v. Burdine 450 U.S. 248 (1981) employer must show legitimate reason.
Action: File EEOC charge within 300-day statute of limitations.
